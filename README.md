# Earth Risk Watch · Australia first

This project is **deliberately Australia-first**.

It is a plain-language public awareness page about planetary and climate-linked risks, with Australian agencies and lived local context prioritised.

## Official warnings always win

This is **not** an official warning system.

Use these first:

- [Bureau of Meteorology](https://www.bom.gov.au/)
- Your **state emergency service** / fire service apps
- Local council and police directions in an emergency

## Why Australia first?

People prepare better when they recognise **their** weather service, **their** fire danger ratings, and **their** last bad summer.

Global jargon-heavy dashboards often fail that test.

## If you are not in Australia — please fork this

The intended model is:

1. Someone who cares about **their country** forks this repo
2. They replace Australian agencies, examples, and prep steps with **local** ones
3. They add real local detail: evacuation culture, emergency numbers, trusted official links, history people in that country actually remember

One serious maintainer per country is enough to start.

Please keep official national warning systems as the top authority in any fork.
