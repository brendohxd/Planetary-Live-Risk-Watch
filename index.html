Not in Australia?

Fork this and build your country’s version

This project is deliberately Australia-first. The idea is simple: people who care about their country should copy this repo and build a local warning and prep site — with local agencies, local history people recognise, and real evacuation and safety info for their places.

One passionate person per country is enough to start. Global dashboards rarely beat a local page that names the right emergency number, the right weather service, and the routes people actually use.
