Nuclear risk — honesty box

For nuclear risk, the useful levers are mostly politics and verified arms limits, not a household kit. For heat, fire, and flood, household prep works. This site will not pretend a go-bag solves nuclear war.

Offline kit · while you still have signal

When Google Maps dies, people panic

This site is not turn-by-turn navigation. Prepare before that day.

  1. Install Organic Maps or OsmAnd and download your state on Wi‑Fi.
  2. Screenshot or print leave-early routes and a suburb street map.
  3. Write on paper: meeting place, two exits, emergency numbers.
  4. Open this site once so the briefing can save offline.
Australia orientation (offline)
If you must leave with no phone maps
  • Leave early in daylight when told.
  • Know two ways out of your street before summer.
  • Drive to a place you already know — not a new GPS pin.
  • Do not drive into smoke or floodwater.
