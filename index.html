Offline kit · do this while you still have signal

When Google Maps dies, people panic

This website is not turn-by-turn navigation. A webpage cannot replace GPS maps if both data and GPS are gone. What works is preparing before that day.

  1. Install an offline map app now — Organic Maps or OsmAnd (free). Open it on Wi‑Fi and download your state / region maps.
  2. Screenshot or print your leave-early routes and a paper street map of your suburb.
  3. Write on paper: meeting place, two exits from your street, state emergency number, neighbour names.
  4. Open this site once on good signal so it can save offline on your phone.
Australia orientation (works offline)

Not for driving. Use only to keep calm and orient. Real routes need offline map apps or paper maps.

If you must leave with no phone maps
  • Leave early in daylight when told — night + smoke + no maps is how people get trapped.
  • Know two ways out of your street before summer.
  • Drive to a named place you already know (town centre, relative, oval) — not a new GPS pin.
  • Take paper: address of meeting point, medical needs, pet plan.
  • Do not drive into smoke or floodwater to “check the app”.

State fire apps still need signal for live warnings. Offline map apps only help with roads you already downloaded.
