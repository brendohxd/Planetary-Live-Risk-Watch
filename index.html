How to read the stages

Monitoring

Background risk. Stay aware; no special action needed beyond normal preparedness.

Elevated

Above normal or regionally important. Worth watching and basic readiness.

Building

Trend is worsening or pressure is accumulating. Plan ahead; effects may compound.

Critical

High or severe concern with serious near-term or systemic implications.
